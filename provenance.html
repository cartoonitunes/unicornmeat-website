I. Origin & Provenance

This certifies that Unicorn Meat (🍖) is an authentic historic Ethereum token, deployed on Ethereum Mainnet in March 2016 by Alex Van de Sande (avsa.eth) — creator of the Ethereum Wallet and Mist Browser, co-creator of ENS, and core Ethereum Foundation developer since 2014.

The token predates the ERC-20 standard and stands among the earliest smart contract tokens ever deployed on Ethereum. It was publicly announced by the official @ethereum Twitter account on April 1, 2016.
